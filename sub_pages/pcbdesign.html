PCB Design

The art of making circuit board

More details

During my STI2D Baccalaureate, I often had to draw, design, and correct electrical diagrams for robots, alarms, and RFID badges, that is why I started to learn how to use KiCad.

After graduating, I went on to study electrical engineering and industrial computing to deepen my knowledge of electronics. During those years, I improved my use of KiCad and also learned how to use LTspice to simulate electrical circuits and Autodesk Eagle to create printed circuit boards.

This knowledge has been useful in creating a robot pilot card and sensor management card (preview), as well as in another audio amplifier card project to modulate/modify and improve an audio signal.
