In addition to my interest in electronics, I sometimes use graphic design software such as Photoshop and After Effects. This comes in handy, as some people ask me to design logos or edit videos. This was the case in 2024, when Harmonie Confort, a construction company specializing in plastering and insulation, needed a new logo for its business cards.

Another example is february 2023, when I was asked to help set up video mapping for HRUN, an association that organizes concerts and provides assistance to people with disabilities.

More recently, unlike video editing, I programmed an entire video animation software program to create drawings of actor placements during performances.
