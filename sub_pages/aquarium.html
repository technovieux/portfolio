Aquarium

the fish are always kept in the best conditions

More details

Alongside my studies, I sometimes take on more personal projects, or projects that people ask me to do. That was the case with this aquarium, which was built for a fishing enthusiast who wanted to keep his fish, except that the aquarium was in the garage, so it was very cold.

I had to manage a pump, heating, and filtration system. In addition to the power supply, I had to manage the control of each piece of equipment with a box that displayed all the information from the sensors (temperature, time, water acidity, etc.) and had an automatic and manual mode.

It was possible to run the systems automatically at a certain time or activate them manually.
