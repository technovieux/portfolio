My passion for theater began in 2021 when I discovered Jean-Paul Rappeneau's adaptation of Cyrano de Bergerac.

It was after that that I wanted to join a theater troupe, which I did in January 2023, allowing me to perform in my first show, a staging of Dossier Brulant by Jacques Barant.

Subsequently, I participated in the implementation of HRUN's video mapping in 2023, and these moments with the production team reinforced my desire to work in the performing arts.

After that, I had to move away to continue my studies, which meant leaving the troupe. Then, in january 2025, I joined RECORDATIO, a sound and light show troupe that performs at historic sites. That same year, I performed in the show “Souviens-toi Cornilly” (Remember Cornilly), staged at Cornilly Abbey, and “Mélusine et le pacte rompu” (Mélusine and the Broken Pact) at the Château de la Recepte in Vouvant.
